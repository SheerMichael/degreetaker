Admission Requirements

  • Must be a graduate of an accredited 4-year high school recognized by the DepEd.
  • Must pass the College Entrance Test (CET) and Aptitude Test for courses like Engineering, Nursing, Criminology and Education.
  • Must submit high school card (or equivalent certification) to the Office of the Dean of Admissions.
  • Must submit a certificate of good moral character from the Principal or Guidance Counsellor of the last school attended.
